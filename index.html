How Does Locate Work?

Locate receives and broadcast reports of missing, displaced and trafficked
persons while working with citizens and stakeholders to help in finding them.

File report

Do you think or know someone that is missing? You do NOT need to wait for 24 hours. REPORT NOW!

Verify

To reduce false calls/reports, Locate verifies the authenticity of every report received.

Share

We rely on you to share photo of missing persons for more visibility and increase the chance we have of finding them.

Disclaimer: Locate is NOT a law enforcement agency and does not monitor criminal activities, make arrests or investigates crime. Locate is a crowd sourcing platform that allows people to easily report missing persons while leveraging on social media to instantly engage citizens and relevant stakeholders to help in the search for missing persons. Nothing in this website constitutes or creates a law enforcement-citizen relationship.
